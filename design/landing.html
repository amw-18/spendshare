How it works

SpendShare simplifies expense sharing with a few easy steps.

1. Split the Bill

Create a group or add individuals to split expenses. Enter the total amount and let SpendShare calculate each person's share.

2. Choose Your Crypto

Select your preferred cryptocurrency for settlement. SpendShare supports a wide range of digital currencies.

3. Get Paid Back

Receive your share of the expenses in your chosen cryptocurrency, directly into your wallet.
